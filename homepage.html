Welcome to the Rhubarb Lip Sync API

The Rhubarb Lip Sync API is a powerful tool for generating accurate lip sync animations for characters in your projects. Whether you're working on games, animations, or other creative endeavors, Rhubarb makes it easy to bring your characters to life.

Features

  • Fast and accurate lip sync generation
  • Supports multiple audio formats
  • Easy integration with your existing workflows
